# Design Brief

## Style: Rainforest Refuge

The design should evoke a sense of peace and tranquility, with elements that resemble a rainforest ecosystem. The atmosphere should be lush and vibrant, with organic curves and natural textures. The visual elements should include plant-based textures, such as leaves and vines, and a color palette that reflects the rich, emerald hues of the rainforest. The design should feel serene and rejuvenating, inviting users to find solace and refuge in the world of turtles.

## Design Tokens

| Aspect | Direction |
|--------|-----------|
| Colors | Lush, vibrant greens with a focus on emerald and olive tones |
| Typography | Friendly, approachable sans-serif fonts |
| Components | Organic curves, natural textures, and subtle shadows |
| Imagery | Whimsical illustrations of turtles in a rainforest ecosystem |

## Theme Settings

- **Theme Color:** green
- **Mode:** dark
- **Border Radius:** 0.3

## App Variant: TurtlePal

> Care for turtles, virtually

This variant focuses on creating a virtual pet experience where users can care for and learn about turtles. It's designed for kids and educational institutions, promoting conservation and responsibility. The app includes interactive games, quizzes, and a virtual habitat for the turtles.

**Target Audience:** Children and educational institutions
**Monetization:** Subscription-based model with in-app purchases for virtual items
**Complexity:** standard
