## Pre-Launch Phase (4-6 weeks before)

### Build Waitlist
- [ ] Create landing page with email capture
- [ ] Offer early access incentive (e.g., lifetime discount, extra features)
- [ ] Set up referral mechanism
- [ ] Target: 500-1,000 signups

### Prepare Content
- [ ] Write 3-5 blog posts for launch week
- [ ] Create demo video (2-3 minutes)
- [ ] Prepare Product Hunt assets
- [ ] Design social media templates
- [ ] Write press release / media kit

### Outreach Prep
- [ ] List of 50 relevant journalists/bloggers
- [ ] List of 100 potential beta testers
- [ ] Connect with influencers in space
- [ ] Identify communities to engage

---

## Launch Week

### Day 1: Soft Launch
- Send access to waitlist (first 100)
- Gather immediate feedback
- Fix critical issues

### Day 2-3: Expand Access
- Open to full waitlist
- Begin social media push
- Personal outreach to contacts

### Day 4: Product Hunt Launch
- Launch on Product Hunt at 12:01 AM PT
- Engage with all comments
- Share on social media
- Email list to support

### Day 5-7: Media Push
- Pitch to journalists
- Post in relevant communities
- Continue engagement
